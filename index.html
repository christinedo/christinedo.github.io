About

Hi, I'm Christine! I'm a UX/UI Designer with an unfettered enthusiasm for problem-solving and a not-yet-fully-realized fascination with frontend and backend development. I wholly believe in the power of iteration and as such, I never stop looking for opportunities for improvement‚Äîwhether it's in a project or just in my daily life. To me, there's nothing more satisfying than seeing or hearing about the impact of what I've helped created.

Even when I'm not actively working on a project, I never stop exploring or learning. You can often find my browser window filled with tabs on a range of design and dev topics: From How-To videos/guides on Angular JS (my current undertaking) to what is essentially all the external links on UsePanda.
